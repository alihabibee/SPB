Reorder Triggers SOP

When and how to prompt patient reorders and clinic restocking

Patient Reorder Triggers
1

Follow-Up Visit

When: Patient returns for any follow-up appointment

If they purchased Rest previously, ask about their experience. If positive, this is a natural moment to suggest a second unit for travel or gifting.

"How's the Rest device working for you? A lot of patients end up getting a second one for travel or for a family member. Interested?"
2

Annual Exam

When: Routine annual comprehensive exam

Check patient history. If they're a Rest user, reinforce the habit. If they tried it in-office but didn't buy, re-offer with updated pricing or offers.

"I see you tried Rest in-office last year but didn't take one home. Still experiencing eye fatigue? We have a new offer that might work better for you."
3

Family Mention

When: Patient mentions family member with similar symptoms

Listen for cues like "my husband has the same problem" or "my daughter is on screens all day." These are natural referral moments.

"You mentioned your daughter has similar issues. We could set her up with her own device — or you could bring her in for a demo next time."

⚠️ Inventory Alert System

Never run out during peak recommendation periods. Set up alerts:

  • Reorder trigger: When inventory drops to 3 units
  • Urgent reorder: When inventory drops to 1 unit
  • Lead time: Allow 5-7 business days for delivery

Clinic Restock Process

1

Check inventory weekly

Designate one staff member to count units every Monday

2

Submit reorder at 3 units

Email orders@umay.care or use partner portal

3

Confirm delivery window

You'll receive tracking within 48 hours of order

4

Verify shipment on arrival

Count units, check for damage, update inventory log
