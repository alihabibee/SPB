"What Would Need to Be True" for These Numbers to Hold

1. Your practice sees 10+ dry eye or screen fatigue patients per week. (DED prevalence: 40–80% of adult patients.)
2. Staff consistently asks the trigger question at intake: "Do your eyes feel tired or strained after screen time?"
3. 10–20% of flagged patients convert at $500–$600. (Industry benchmark: 30–60% for cash-pay.)
4. Patients scan QR code at checkout — those who skip setup have 3× lower adherence, increasing return risk.
5. Reorder inventory at 3 units remaining. Lead time: 5–7 business days.
6. No competing at-home thermal therapy enters the market at comparable compliance rates in the next 12 months.
