Founder Story Script

The "Why Now" Narrative — For podcasts, intro calls, video

The Origin · 30 seconds
"I started Umay because of a personal experience. I was diagnosed with severe dry eye by my co-founder, Dr. Sharmin — she's an optometrist. She recommended warm compresses. I tried everything — microwaveable masks, hot towels, the works. Nothing stuck."
The Insight · 30 seconds
"One day, I was testing a prototype and there was a bug — it started pulsing between warm and cool instead of staying warm. Something about that rhythm felt different. I couldn't stop using it.

We realized: the bug was the feature. The pulsing thermal therapy was activating something the static heat wasn't."
The Validation · 30 seconds
"We ran a government-backed study with 500 people. 87% reported improved eye comfort. 70% reported better sleep. And here's the kicker: 84% were still using it daily months later.

Compare that to warm compresses, where compliance drops below 50% within weeks."
The Market · 30 seconds
"There are 104 million people in North America with dry eye symptoms. They spend billions on drops that don't work because the underlying fatigue isn't addressed. We're the first to treat the fatigue layer — and we're doing it through eye care professionals, not direct-to-consumer."
The Ask · 15 seconds
"We're looking for clinic partners who want to give their patients something that actually works at home. We're not trying to replace what you do — we're trying to protect it."
