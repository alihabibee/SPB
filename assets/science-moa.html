Why Warm Compresses Underperform

Moist heat = evaporative cooling

Wet compresses lose therapeutic temperature within 60 seconds. The evaporation actively counteracts lid warming benefits.

Microwaveables overshoot

Variable heating creates temps above 43°C (injury risk) or below therapeutic threshold. No sustained delivery.

Rest: dry heat, precise control

Non-moist device delivers consistent 39-41°C—within therapeutic range, below injury threshold. No reheating.
